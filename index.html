About Me

Hi, I'm Jackie Watson - a technical games artist and generalist developer with a background in computer games development.
I specialize in both 3D modelling and gameplay programming, with a passion for crafting rich and immersive interactive worlds.
After earning my degree, I've participated in multiple game jams and continued growing my skills, specifically in Unity and Blender.
I love combining art and code to bring imaginative concepts to life.
I'm currently based in San Marino and open to remote or freelance oppurtunities.
